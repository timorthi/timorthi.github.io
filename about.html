About

My name is Timothy Ng (pronounced ing). I am currently living in Los Angeles, California where I'm in my junior year at California State University, Northridge pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. If you'd like to get in touch with me, you can e-mail me. You can also find me on GitHub, Twitter, and LinkedIn.

I strive to enhance the world through code. In my spare time, I design and develop programs that have a positive impact on the community using technologies like Python, Java and JavaScript. You can check out my portfolio to see what I've created so far.

Video Games & e-Sports

Video games hold a special place in my heart. Curious ten-year-old me happened upon the source code of a private server of a game I loved. A devoted player of this game at the time, I decided to fire up my own server instance, ardently tweaking the many variables in the game to my liking. Personally, I would describe this moment — the moment I understood the power of code — to be a situation akin to Neo seeing The Matrix for the first time: a mind-blowing, mesmerizing point in time that ignited a fire in me for programming that still burns to this day.

While I no longer play many games myself, I love to watch people play them especially on a competitive level. I am a huge fan of professional gaming, otherwise known as e-Sports. I work with the National Collegiate e-Sports League (NCeSPA). We host competitions for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, League of Legends, and DotA 2 where active college students compete for the title of Collegiate Champions and perhaps pave their way to the professional level.

Fitness

I believe in healthy living. I adopt a wholesome lifestyle by trying out new ways of staying active — from playing paintball competitively to doing freestyle inline skating. I am also fond of experimenting with different types of meal compositions for optimal nutritional intake.

Aside from that, I am a huge soccer fan. Soccer, in my opinion, is a great way to maintain a good fitness level but have fun at the same time. If you don't see me on the field playing with my friends, you'd probably find me watching a game involving my favorite soccer club, FC Bayern Munich.
